# ADR-B-005 — Visual Language Avoids Traditional Insurance Symbols

**Marca:** EcoSeg Seguros

**Identificador:** ADR-B-005

**Categoria:** Brand Architecture Decision Record

**Status:** Accepted

**Versão:** 1.0

---

## Decision

A linguagem visual evita símbolos tradicionais de seguradoras.

## Context

Formalizes as a standalone decision record the restriction already established across `VIS-001 §1.3` and expanded in `DES-006` (§Restrições Ampliadas): shields, locks, safes, umbrellas, handshakes, and — per `DES-006`'s expansion — hands protecting objects and smiling-people-pointing-at-charts stock photography. This ADR does not add new restrictions; it gives the existing restriction its own citable decision record, independent of the longer visual-language document it lives inside.

## Consequences

Any future logo, icon set, or illustration proposal is checked against this ADR directly, not only against the fuller `DES-006` document — a shorter reference for reviewers who need the rule without the full rationale.

## Traceability

Formalizes: `VIS-001 §1.3`, `DES-006 §Restrições Ampliadas`.

Affects: `DES-006` (Icons, Illustrations, Photography sections), future logo/icon work.
