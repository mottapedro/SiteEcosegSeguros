# ADR-B-001 — Logo Represents Comprehension, Not Protection

**Marca:** EcoSeg Seguros

**Identificador:** ADR-B-001

**Categoria:** Brand Architecture Decision Record

**Status:** Accepted

**Versão:** 1.0

---

## Decision

O logotipo não representa proteção; representa compreensão do contexto.

## Context and Reconciliation Note

`VIS-001 §VIS-001.3A.3` framed the fundamental-unit hypothesis as "um núcleo protegido por camadas de contexto" — a formulation that names _both_ protection (of the núcleo) _and_ context (the camadas) as part of what the symbol communicates. This ADR narrows that framing: the logo itself is not a protection symbol (which would risk collapsing back into shield/lock iconography, already vetoed in `VIS-001 §1.3` and `DES-006`) — it represents the _comprehension_ that makes protection possible, per the Progress Protection Philosophy's own sequence (`BD-003 §6`, Decision Model: Compreensão precedes Decisão precedes Proteção).

This is a refinement, not a contradiction: protection remains the philosophy's ultimate outcome (`BD-003 §2`), but the logo — the single symbol standing in for the whole brand — represents the step that makes EcoSeg different (mapping and understanding context), not the generic outcome every insurer claims to offer.

## Consequences

- Logo exploration (`VIS-001.4` / `DS-004`'s Identity Exploration) should draw from "revealing structure" (`VIS-001 §1.1`, Painel B — Ciência) rather than containment/shield imagery, even discreet ones.
- This does not relax the anti-cliché restrictions in `DES-006` — shields, locks, umbrellas remain vetoed regardless of this ADR.

## Traceability

Affects: future logo design work (`DS-004` / `VIS-001.4`, not yet started).
